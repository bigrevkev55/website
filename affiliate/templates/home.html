Thomas Boxing Federation

About

The Thomas Boxing Federation (TBF) is a fictional boxing federation consisting of two weight divisions. The Heavweight Division is the top level of the TBF, while the Middleweight Division contains several of the most beloved fighters in boxing history. The TBF uses the game Title Bout II (link in menu) to play out its matches and tournaments.
